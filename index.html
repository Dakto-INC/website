Main Info

Dakto INC is a Survival and Creative Multiplayer Minecraft Server running on Spigot 1.21.4 with a player cap of 20 players. It has been running since 17 April 2024 nonstop.

There’s also an Anarchy Server on Paper 1.21.4 with a similar player cap. We hope you enjoy this alternate experience!

How to connect to Dakto INC?

Java: You can join Dakto INC with an official/cracked Minecraft Java Edition client on 1.7 to 1.21.6 and join minecraft.daktoinc.co.uk.
Eaglercraft: You can join Dakto INC through Eaglercraft on any version from 1.5.2 to 1.12.2 and add the server ip wss://eagercraft.daktoinc.co.uk.

Bedrock: You can join Dakto INC with an official/cracked Minecraft Bedrock Edition account on 1.21.80 to 1.21.90 and join minecraft.daktoinc.co.uk with port 19132

Make sure to always read the rules when joining the server.

Where are the rules?

You can find them in-game or on our Discord server. We are fairly open—Xray, Freecam, and Fullbright are all allowed. However, no kill-aura, reach, or other major cheats.

No Discord?

You can use the IRC channel or websites like Dynmap or Minetest-style chat portals with WEBIRC support.

Places of Interest

  • Original Spawn

    The original spawn of Dakto INC. This spawn was based on the film Stargate. There’s a railway that can take you thousands of blocks east if you want to build far away.

  • Get Stuffs Farm

    This farm was built by the admins and is used as a quick way for people (permission required) to get certain materials. It’s very efficient and able to get you stacks in a few seconds.

  • Corrupted Chunks

    This area of land was created due to a corruption when testing 1.13 on the server which lead to block the entire area until further notice. Recently, people have asked to open up this land again.
